###Comprehension_Questions###
------------------------------
1. What is the difference between static and dynamic task allocation?
Static: Tasks are assigned in advance (round-robin).
Dynamic: Tasks are assigned to workers as they become free, which improves load balancing.

2. What are the advantages of a master-worker model compared to peer-to-peer communication?
.Centralized control
.Easier task scheduling
.Better monitoring and logging
.Simple error handling

3. What challenges arise as the number of workers grows?
.Network congestion
.Synchronization issues
.More complex error handling
.Task scheduling becomes harder
